Frequently Asked Questions

What is the SpaceX Dragon?

The SpaceX Dragon is a reusable spacecraft designed to carry cargo and crew to the International Space Station.

How many times has Dragon flown?

As of now, the Dragon spacecraft has completed dozens of successful missions.

What makes Dragon unique?

Dragon is the first private spacecraft to dock with the International Space Station and is known for its reusability, safety features, and advanced technology.
