History of the SpaceX Dragon

The SpaceX Dragon was first launched in 2010 as the world's first commercial spacecraft to be recovered successfully from orbit. In 2012, it became the first private spacecraft to dock with the International Space Station.

Its development has been a testament to SpaceX's commitment to innovation, safety, and reliability in space travel.
